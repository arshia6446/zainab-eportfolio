Criterion D – Evaluating

Reflecting on artwork and growth

Paragraph 1 — Evaluating My Artwork

I think my mini dress and corset show the themes of happiness, transformation, and softness well. The lavender colours and delicate fabrics create a calm and gentle feeling, while the butterfly-inspired shape represents growth. Experimenting with different materials helped me choose ones that matched the mood I wanted. The floral elements strengthen the symbolism, and the layering effect gives the design an airy look. If I were to improve it, I would add a bit more contrast between structured and soft parts to make it visually stronger.

Paragraph 2 — Evaluating My Growth

This project helped me understand how to connect emotions with visual design. I got better at using mood boards to guide my choices and combining research on colours, culture, and materials to create a clear message. If I did this again, I would try more advanced fabric printing techniques to add deeper texture. Overall, I learned how important intention is in design and how visual elements can communicate feelings, which will help me in future creative projects.
