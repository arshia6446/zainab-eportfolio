Criterion C – Creating

Artistic intention and final concept

Paragraph 1 — Artistic Intention

My goal was to design a mini dress and corset that visually express happiness through themes of transformation, softness, and nature. Butterflies represent growth, flowers represent purity and beauty, and lavender tones create a calm feeling. I chose light materials like lace and organza because they move gently and create an airy look. Adding small gold details brought warmth into the design and helped highlight the emotional meaning behind it.

Paragraph 2 — Final Concept

When I moved from planning to the final design, I shaped the dress so it would resemble soft butterfly wings through curved and layered edges. I added floral prints or embroidery to represent inner joy and natural beauty. Gold accents were placed carefully to bring elegance and warmth. Every choice—shape, colour, fabric, and detail—connected back to my research on visual culture, designers like Phạm Đăng Anh Thư, Marimekko, Finnish design ideas, and colour psychology. Altogether, the design communicates happiness and transformation the way I intended.
